ALLIANCE WAR – TENGU – EVENT DETAILS:

Deep within a cavern at the precipice of the tallest mountain in all the Shattered Kingdoms, an ancient, supernatural spirit stirs in the darkness. Kept away from contact with all living creatures for generations, TENGU begins his descent, unwavering in his intention of bringing war and chaos to all who reside in the realm.
