ALLIANCE WAR – Satyrs Festival – Dionysus FINISH+

STARTS: 5th August, 15:00 UTC

ENDS: 9th August, 09:00 UTC

Dionysus, the god of Festivity, makes a revelrous return to the Shattered Kingdoms to answer his family’s call for aid! Celebrate the occasion with two powerful relics: the Thyrsus, an ancient staff which symbolized prosperity, and a Golden 5* relic Godfrey’s Poleaxe.

* FIREBALL, LIGHTNING, LIGHTNING STORM, POISON CLOUD

** SHIELD, RAGE, FREEZE, TERROR

*** 2 FOR MILITIA, 2 FOR ARCHER AND 2 FOR SPEARMEN

The Satyrs 

Dionysus calls forth his eccentric and loyal followers into battle. Blessed with the power of Dionysus the Satyrs long for battle and always celebrate their victories.
