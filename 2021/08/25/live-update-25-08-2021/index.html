ALLIANCE WAR – The Hound of Hades – Cerberus FINISH+

STARTS: 26th August, 15:00 UTC

ENDS: 30th August, 09:00 UTC

Cerberus, the beast that guards the gates of the underworld, hungers for blood as he stands in the path of those that threaten his domain. He makes his return to the Shattered Kingdoms with a brand new synergy relic, Cycladic Idol, as well as the Golden 5* Relic Forsetti’s Axes.
