ALLIANCE WAR – Tarhunz – EVENT DETAILS:

STARTS: 12th November, 15:00 UTC
ENDS: 16th November, 09:00 UTC

As dawn broke and sunlight scattered across the battlefield, a land strewn with the bodies of Unak tribesmen emerged. The ground, razed by fire, was now unrecognisable to the flora and fauna that once thrived there.

Magmataur, hearing of this infernal battle, arrived at the edge of the battlefield and approached the burnt remains. The beast paused as he took in the scene, overwhelmed with a sense of sadness, guilt, and then eventually…fear. It was inconceivable to him that the Unak – forebearers of the flame – be destroyed by their own powers. Magmataur turned away, unable anymore to look upon the death and destruction. As he did so, he spotted a strange battalion of Grenadiers in the distance and began his approach. 

As he edged closer, Magmataur knew something was wrong. These warriors had been corrupted, their minds and bodies now at the whim of someone with immense power and control over Nexus energy. Through the smoke and fumes, he spotted a gargantuan Titan towering over them, holding a great scimitar made with the bones of an ancient Dragon. Magmataur prepared his blades – ‘this Titan is no ally to the Unaks’, he thought.

Magmataur rushed the group with the rage and heat of an erupting volcano, and upon reaching the Titan, immediately sunk his blades into their torso. A brief moment of deafening silence fell across the land, and as Magmataur looked up, he was confronted not by the pained expression of an impaled man, but instead of a maniacal grin across the Titan’s face. He quickly retracted his blades and took up a defensive position, only to then bear witness to the wounds healing right before his eyes. Overwhelmed by the intensity of this Titan’s aura, he began to feel his own flame-riddled body cool, solidifying the magma that has long-flowed through his veins.

‘Fire may have given you life, but I, Tarhunz, am the only one who has truly mastered the flame!’

Tarhunz, the legendary Luwian god and Fire Berserker, arrives in the Shattered Kingdoms with his corrupted Grenadiers and debuffing Aura. Our SPPs will receive this Titan later today.

Corrupted Grenadiers

Tarhunz calls Corrupted Grenadiers into the reinforcement zone with reduced Ranged Attack distance, but +400% Health, +600% Melee Fire Damage, increased movement speed and unit size. Their Health and Melee damage make them formidable defenses for the frontline of battle.
