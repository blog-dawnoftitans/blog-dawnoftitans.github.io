1.42 UPDATE

We are excited to announce that the next update to Dawn of Titans, version 1.42, will be released to players soon.

We have a number of changes and new content coming to the game which we will reveal over the coming weeks, starting with the update below.

ALLIANCE WAR – Apostate Inferno

STARTS: 10th June, 15:00 UTC

ENDS: 14th June, 09:00 UTC

NEPHELINIS, the Fire Archmage, unleashes her full power upon the Shattered Kingdoms with a new synergy relic. The Pyromancer’s Locket, given to Nephelinis by Everdure after the fall of the Archmage tower, is a badge of honor bestowed upon the highest ranking Pyromancer. Armed with this relic Nephelinis and her army can pierce through the armor of any foe.

  • A Fire Elemental is a LVL 60 Guardian with +50% All Elemental Resist. Any Skills or Relics that boost Fire Titan stats will also boost Fire Elementals.
  • On Titan attack, Nephelinis now has a 50% chance to -4% Enemy Ranged Resist on the enemies she attacks (Max 20). This change is retroactive.
